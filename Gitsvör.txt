1. Fyrir hvað stendur skammstöfunin RAM? Útskýrðu hvað RAM er. 
Random-access memory and is the place in the compututing device where operating system, application programs and data in current use are kept so they can be quickly reached by the device´s processor

2. Fyrir hvað stendur skammstöfunin ROM? Útskýrðu hvað ROM er.
Read only memory is used in computers and other electronic devices, data stored in rom can only be modifed slowly with difficulty or not at all,and is mainly used to store firmware.

4. Fyrir hvað stendur skammstöfunin ESD, hvað er það og hvaða leiðir höfum við til að
verjast því þegar unnið er með tölvubúnað?
Electrostatic discharge can occur when there is a buildup of an electric charge that exists on a surface which comes into contact with another differently charged surface, 
the way to prevent this from happening is to use grounded mats on workbenches, grounded floor mats in the working area, using an antistatic wrist strap when working on computers and by keeping all the components in antistatic bags until you are ready to install them.

5. Hvað þarf að hafa í huga við val á spennugjöfum?
That the power supply provides enough power for the installed components and allow for other components that may be added at a later time.

7. Hver er munurinn á opinn (e. open ended) og lokaðri (closed ended) spurningu?
Nefnið dæmi um báðar gerðir af spurningum.
A closed ended question is a question you can only answer with a yes or a no such as, can you turn the computer on? or is the computer connected to an energy outlet?
While open ended questions are ones that require more then one word answers such as, what components do you have installed on your computer?

8. Hvað eru jaðartæki?
A component that is not installed directly into the computer such as a USB device which is used to store data, webcam, mouse and keyboard.
